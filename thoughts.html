What drives me?

All my life, I have been doing things out excitement for the sake of enjoying the human experience. I was not really aware of what I was doing, I was just excited rather than driven. With time I realised that I need a cause, something to fight for, giving a sense to my existence to make this world better no more shadowing away, I want to stand out, help and contribute with people to solve real world problems. My goal is to solve one of the greatest humans problem: Energy. An energy-abundant world is what I thrive for and I think the only way to make it happen is by making nuclear fusion happen. I don't want to be a billionaire, but I don't want to hide in the shadows, I want to stand out, leave my mark on earth.
